# ☕ Monday Coffee Expansion Analysis

## Project Overview


This SQL project analyzes coffee sales data across multiple cities to identify the best locations for business expansion.

The project includes database design, data import, SQL analysis, and business recommendations based on customer behavior, revenue, and market potential.
![image](https://github.com/adarshvatspandey/Monday_Coffee_Expansion_sql/blob/def18c6e0b3cbaf998bd60d9930b7c75daa08cd8/logo.png)
## Database Schema
![image](https://github.com/adarshvatspandey/Monday_Coffee_Expansion_sql/blob/def18c6e0b3cbaf998bd60d9930b7c75daa08cd8/ERD.png)

The database consists of four tables:

* City
* Customers
* Products
* Sales

Relationships are maintained using Primary Keys and Foreign Keys.

## Business Questions Solved

1. Estimated coffee consumers by city
2. Total revenue in Q4 2023
3. Product sales performance
4. Average sales per customer
5. Market size estimation
6. Top 3 products by city
7. Customer segmentation
8. Rent vs revenue analysis
9. Monthly sales growth
10. Market potential analysis

## Key Insights

### Pune

* Highest revenue generation
* Strong customer spending
* Low rent per customer

### Delhi

* Largest coffee consumer market
* High customer base
* Good expansion opportunity

### Jaipur

* High customer acquisition
* Affordable rent
* Strong average sales
